saw you on the street

today

they say you are back

same old face, same old way

you smiled

but not at me

this old street



I left your last letter unanswered

I thought you were not going anywhere

and we will be the same when I see you again


smiled not at me

waited for someone else

I never thought that would burn

why didn’t you see me?


for long, I stopped dreaming

but there was always your face

on the other sides of the street we were

each time I almost crossed towards you

the morning comes
