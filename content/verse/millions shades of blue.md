millions shades of blue
only one that lives in my memories
only one that spoke to me
only one could evoke those tears

that one shade of blue
closed for eternity
that one shade of blue
forever kept the past seem unreal
that one shade of blue
bleached the world to shades of grey
all these shades I have gone through
cannot match the one shade I look for
many shades have been seen
eternity of others to come
the one shade that matters
may never be found
