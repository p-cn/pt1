underneath my feet,
the ground burns
underneath my breath,
my lips crack bleeding
underneath my words,
there are tears already dried
from inside my mind,
the world fades to an insatiable grey
I can walk crossing these blank faces
I can open up an incomplete grin
I can touch without feeling attached
without thinking, still I can live
the pain been living inside never left
is the only thing I can feel
I stop thinking about the day
when my limbs refuse to move,
my heart skips uncountable beats,
and my brain fails to amaze itself
did you know, they all said
after losing you,
all I can do is to wait to die the second time
eternity ended
while this waiting feels endless
