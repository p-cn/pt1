look there, you died
the world lives on
another day was too much
another hour was only painful
rational you, with your heart touched
emotional you, with your mind thought it all through
and the vast ocean opens to hold you in
there is narrow space in my heart that remains
you can be unburdened
the feet no longer hold you to the ground
look there, I lived
watching the world forgetting you
cars passing, light running
sounds breaking, smell invading
inside out my feelings up close
I can no longer feel you
and each day passes,
like you never even existed
how dare they forget you
how dare they talk about you
could I ever die at all
when all my hopes burned to your ground
wondering where I might go
wondering what I might want to see
I closed my eyes
away from the beauty you showed
I walked my path
away from the past that I have
in which I thought I’d be living
he said that I am only rot inside
because the parasite looks me
is amazingly alive
