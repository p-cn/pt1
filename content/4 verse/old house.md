somewhere in this heart, I think found love
somewhere on this earth, I found you
in the old realms of life, we met
gone the sun
gone those hopes
cold nights crept in less and less strange
when I touch the ground, I can hear their noises
Time vibrated
Dreams shot down
even the loudest peace became silenced
and time had only stopped for those blackouts
I remember how mesmerizing the sound of your name was
only to feel the blunt blade it is
now cutting in the middle of the night
smoke flies and whispered words in breath
could not bring those days back
the good part in me
disappeared without a trace
writing down your name
they won’t ever make sense
why taking me away
only to have pushed me back
and made me watch these memories to decay
