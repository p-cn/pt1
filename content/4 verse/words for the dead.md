I walked the same street again
only to find your shadow erased
mine lingered a moment
not long
the hell I once betrayed
the place I came from

do you hear them singing in the air anymore?
I bathed in sunset light
where memories of you burned like stars
my feet grew heavy
walking from what I was meant to be
stole that moment to smile
you were holding my hand

all the things we saw,
all that had been,
still pull me back to you
these are things long lost,
things I loved,
but you remain
an unreal moment in my view.

too much to believe in,
too little to live in.
your shadow comes again in my time.
whenever I close my eyes,
I run back to you,
only you and I existed.

we said eternity was disgust,
remember, when I hear this song,
my hand will grow cold,
my heart turn still.
your tenderness, my sentiment
once we were away together
from here
