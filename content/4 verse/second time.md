underneath my feet,<br>the ground burns<br>underneath my breath,<br>my lips crack bleeding<br>underneath my words,<br>there is life already dried<br>

inside my mind,<br>the world faded to an insatiable grey<br>

I saw these blank faces<br>I grinned incompletely<br>my touch didn't stay<br>

without feeling, still I can live<br>

the pain inside never left<br>though I stopped thinking about that day<br>my heart forces yet another beat,<br>I stopped counting the days<br>

losing you,<br>all I do is to wait to die the second time<br>our eternity long gone<br>while this waiting feels endless<br>
