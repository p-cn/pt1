Very few times we could feel compensated for the past we have lost. When past is a tragedy and there is nothing we can do to take that part of our soul trapped there, the days we’d live in shall be haunted with the hours we never spent. Nothing could ever be worth more for that pursuit. Time passed kills the innocent us. Memories became glimpses of drunk moments and drunk moments became the truest of ourselves. Somewhere, we left for the person we never thought we could be. That past self looks at us each day passed, is a cruel reminder which we became used to after times. One day, we might forget it all. One day, this current self makes sense. Who we really are, is only a dream


“Because life is a dream.

We’ll wake up when we die.

Killing ourselves is cheating.
