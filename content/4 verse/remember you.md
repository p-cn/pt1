The last time I saw you, is when you’re not here anymore. I wondered how I could live by when I will see your face everywhere I go. You made me go on asking if there was anything that important to us.

“There won’t be Heaven and life will bring you to your knees.”

Do you remember when you held me into sleep, you asked me why we walked so far that going back and it’s not home anymore, but a mistake, walked down the wrong path. You would say: “Do you remember now?”

I remember now. I was that extravagant child before knowing you. I chose to love making people suffer. I ignored all those philosophies of life but loving in such way. I walked hand in hand with him throughout the most beautiful parts of my life; I found myself and lost my dream then. I expect nobody to understand but perhaps I had too much that I had to let they go.

I dreamed, seeing him standing alone next to that ice lake, looking to my horizon but he was no longer holding my hand. I was not sad, for all the best had come and gone, without crushing my heart as I feared.

I remember now. I walked alone on many roads of this city; they grew tired of me; I decided to freeze my own heart for so long without wanting to wake up. Day passed as I looked to the other side of my window, waiting from something I didn’t know of. I was that tough, I was that obvious; but not comprehend what was awaiting, knowing what I would do.

I dreamed of myself flying in the middle of that vast sky, or holding that gun killing people who chased me. I dreamed of myself holding his hand, floating to another side of this universe. I dreamed of cities I’ve never been to, roads I’ve never walked, planets I’ve never set foot on.

I remember now. The day I met you was so extraordinary as things came to show themselves onto my life to welcome you. Boring classes, drizzling rain and that odd feeling sitting at the airport became fluttering in my chest. I imagined you exactly as you are and that was one of those billion times I did so, it came true, perfectly each line of each word you have said. You walked that extravagant way that I’d had, you woke people as you were born to do so, you eased the hearts that thought they were happy, out of their troubles.

I dreamed of myself drowning in thoughts about you and nobody could have known how  easy those ordinary wishes could be fulfilled. So, I did really fall for you and seems like you even knew before I did. You never talked about it and I only know you did the right thing just now. You let me in that vivid world that only those who are sinful can find; you sheltered me from myself, prevented things from being messed up but I was able to go much wilder in any thoughts. You were right when you said, “When we collide, it’s more like we laughed at ourselves then completed them.”

Those evil good deeds turned out to be a joke and we together wrote our own bible. I would lean on you ‘til the end of the days to listen to your sighs. You would walk with me on every street to erase my lonely shadow.

When I knew I walked too far, you were there to wake me to my sleep. There is truly nothing so crazy or so wild. You gave me a new name, a new identity and a new self. I believed I can be complete because of you.

I chose ways to see you again and again. I know I am a self-destructible thing now. But what is life if one more step is all that matters, to see your face in those summer days. I lost in dreams which are only places I can hold your hand, I will be listening in those old times, like nothing ever changes, reality is a nonsense joke, and time finally is stopped by me.

I walked on the same street again, just to know your shadow has been unpainted. I bathed under the sunset, where memories on you shine like stars. Each time, you are holding my hand, we ran across these trees on the sidewalk. They cut them all down.

Once or twice when time passes by, I saw your smile again, not in this city, but your own Manchester. I walked to you, vigorously, like in heaven and we were walking on those streets one more time.

Perhaps I’ll see you again, even you’re not here or there anymore.
