I did not get to bury you with my hands
and when you turned away from me,
that was not good bye
memories
wasted and unchanged
there’s the world so big around us
yet too small to contain you
each day I weep for the unsaid feelings
ones that I denied to have
might have changed it all
might have undone my faults
hopes are unreal
only bitterness on my tongue
that I can taste
words for your suffering were all fake
nobody was with you in the last moments
did you think of me at all?
every second, every breath
habit, melodies, belongings,
noises, drinks, cloths, drugs
I tried them all new
yet they all fake
destined to walk alone, you said
no such thing
