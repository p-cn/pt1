Because when I leave this world, I will bring your image with me, and your smile and the feeling of your hands. You were not my lover nor my friend. You are the spark that I have been searching for and knowing you have to be broken was enough. I can’t live with the thought that one day you will change and so would I. Things that we have spent together can be all lies and wrong but they won’t matter. I will be with you, never too far away because between us it’s the feeling that counts. You will love me and I will not stop thinking of you.

Whatever it is called, a tragic passing of a man who failed to find his way in this world, or a self-destruction of a cursed soul, he was there with us, with me. And he would have, could have in another world where all the dots are free and bridges are rebuilt for good.
