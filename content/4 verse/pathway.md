Losing touch with reality and inability to cope with it renders me numb sometimes. Each time I reach the bottom, I feel like another part of me is lost. And, I am pretty sure that the day I don’t feel anything anymore, is the day I lose it all.

But does anything that we ever have, actually matter?

We crave love, we want things, but never think about what we need. Loneliness is the basic condition of each human and we underestimate it while we try to achieve things that are unstable and in the moment.

I wait for the day when I am able to look at them in the eyes and say that they have been wrong and astray for too long. But that means I am also reminding them it is impossible to turn back. I did not have to try to love them. It’s only too hard for me to live in the same reality they have chosen. They can say all about how I am irrelevant to material world but they forget that they are a part of me, too. I know I one day I’ll have to leave and they wouldn’t even remember me that long.
